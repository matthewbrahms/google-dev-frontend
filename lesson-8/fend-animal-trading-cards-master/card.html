Site Reliability Engineer

SRE's generally are found in packs of 2 and solve really interesting problems of scale and uptime.

  • Scientific Name: Locum fidus architectus
  • Average Length: Size will vary
  • Average Lifespan: 2-5 years
  • Habitat: Meetups, Conferences, and occassionally a place of employment

Site Reliability Engineers are highly regarded and very hard to find in the wild. Common areas of expertise are: containers, container management, source code management, observability, Linux, metrics (i.e. uptime, latency, errors, throughput), and culture. Typically one can find a good SRE embedded in a development team near them!
